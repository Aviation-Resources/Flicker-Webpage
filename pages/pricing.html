Honest Pricing

We believe the best price is no price. Wherever it's possible, Flicker runs AI directly on your device — keeping features free, fast, and private. When that's not possible, we charge only what it costs us, plus a small margin to keep the lights on.

Edge AI

Most of Flicker's intelligence runs locally on your device using on-device models. No servers, no usage caps, no bill — just instant results that never leave your phone.

Cloud AI

A few features — currently just high-quality image generation — need cloud-scale models that can't run on a phone. We pass through the provider's actual cost and add a small margin. You only pay when you choose to keep the result.

Today this applies to image generation only. If we ever add another paid feature, the rule stays the same: at cost, plus a small margin, paid only when you keep the output.
